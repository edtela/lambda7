Formula Structure

General Form

\[ m = c_5 \pi^5 + c_4 \pi^4 + c_3 \pi^3 + c_2 \pi^2 + \text{correction} \]

Coefficient Rules

\(c_5\) = 6 + |S| Strangeness determines the leading power
\(c_4\) = 6 (decuplet) or 0/1 (octet) Spin-3/2 marker
\(c_3, c_2\) Small integers Fine structure within each level

Correction Types

Type 1: Pure Lorentz

\( \frac{k}{5} e^{-\pi} \)

Proton: \(\frac{4}{5}e^{-\pi}\)

Type 2: Omega-like

\( \frac{k}{5}(e^{-\pi} - \pi + c) \)

Omega: \(\frac{6}{5}(e^{-\pi} - \pi)\)

Type 3: Angular

\( \frac{a}{\pi} \) or \( a\pi + b \)

Neutron: \(\frac{8}{\pi}\)

Type 4: q-Calculus

\( \frac{1}{[2]_\pi} = \frac{1}{\pi + 1} \)

Ξ−: includes \(\frac{1}{[2]_\pi}\)
